Glenview Dairy Bar

Serving Glenview since 1955, Glenview Dairy Bar is a family walk-up soft-serve window. Known for its soft-serve, milkshakes, and other frozen treats, Dairy Bar is a family spot open from late March through early fall. Offering dip-cone flavors ranging from chocolate to peanut butter to cake batter, Dairy Bar's classic chocolate and vanilla soft serve flavors can be doctored up however a customer pleases. Be sure to stop for a cone if you happen to be in the area while in season!

Located at: 1015 Harlem Avenue Glenview, IL 60025.
