Waterfront Activities

Glencoe Beach, located in Glencoe, Illinois, offers more than just a scenic view of Lake Michigan. It is home to a pier, beach house, sprayground, sun shelters, beach rentals, events and classes, life guards on duty, sand volleyball, and a complimentary beach cart to assist patrons with accessing the beach. During the off-season, the beach is open to vistors without a season pass from sunrise-sunset.
